Our story

It all started with a painter, a singer, a garage, and a dream.
In 2015, two art school graduates from UW-Green Bay decided to open a studio space in their garage for the local art department and casually offered hot beverages for the working students.
Here at the Loose Leaf Cafe, we continue to serve local creatives and offer a safe and relaxing environment to the community.

Our Mission

The Loose Leaf Tea House is an artist owned cafe located in the heart of Green Bay, Wisconsin since 2020.
We pride ourselves in our hand crafted brewed tea made from locally sourced, all-natural ingredients.
The Loose Leaf holds space for local artists to display and sell their art in-house.
All profits made from art sales go directly back to the artists.
Our mission is to support local artists and to hold a safe space for self expression and creative pursuits.
