Ladybugs!
*Fun Fact*

What is a Ladybug?

Ladybugs, also called ladybird beetles in Europe, are not bugs at all, but beetles. They belong to the Coccinellidae family.
There are nearly 6,000 species of ladybugs in the world. There are about 450 species in North America.

Lifecycle

  1. Egg
  2. Larva
  3. Pupa
  4. Adult
  5. Metamorphosis

Diet

  • Aphids
  • Mites
  • Insect eggs
  • Nectar
  • Mildew
